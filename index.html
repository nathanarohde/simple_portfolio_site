Chico's Product Typeahead

UI Design

Challenge:

Chico's wanted to incorporate a product typeahead into their existing site design.

Action:

Took advantage of Chico's excellent modeling and photography work and styled the typeahead to display similarly to the search and collection page with reduced information. Ideally this would allow customer to curate their search to a higher degree before arriving at the search page or find specific product in the typeahead itself. Also added a drop layer to eliminate distractions and help user focus on typeahead content. Final design was negotiated with Chico's marketing department.

Result:

Typeahead still in use a year after implementation.
